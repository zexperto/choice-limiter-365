Privacy Policy

Choice Limiter 365  |  Last updated: June 7, 2026

Overview

Choice Limiter 365 is a Google Forms™ Add-on that allows form owners to set response limits on individual answer choices. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly.

Data We Access

Choice Limiter 365 requests the following Google OAuth scopes:

  • forms.currentonly – to read and update the Google Forms™ form you have open.
  • script.container.ui – to display the configuration sidebar inside Google Forms™.
  • script.external_request – to communicate with our licensing service to verify your subscription status.
  • script.scriptapp – to create and manage time-based triggers that monitor form responses.
  • userinfo.email – to identify your Google account for licensing and support purposes.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit the content of your form questions, respondent answers, or any personally identifiable information from your form respondents. All form processing happens within Google's infrastructure via Google Apps Script™.

Subscription Data

If you use a paid plan, we may store your Google account email address solely to validate your subscription. This data is used only for licensing verification and is never sold or shared with third parties.

Data Retention

Subscription and licensing data is retained only as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.

Third-Party Services

Choice Limiter 365 operates entirely within Google Workspace™ (Google Apps Script™ and Google Forms™). We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Our add-on is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from minors.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
