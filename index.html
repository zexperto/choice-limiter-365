How It Works

1

Install Choice Limiter 365 from the Google Workspace Marketplace.

2

Open your Google Form and click Extensions → Choice Limiter 365 → Configure Limits.

3

Set a response limit for each answer choice you want to cap.

4

Save and share your form. Limits are enforced automatically in real time.
