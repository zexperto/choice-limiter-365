What is Choice Limiter 365?

Choice Limiter 365 is a Google Forms Add-on that lets you set response limits on individual answer choices — perfect for sign-ups, seat reservations, and event registrations.

Common Questions

How do I set a limit on a choice?

Open your Google Form, click the Add-ons menu, select Choice Limiter 365, then click Configure Limits. The sidebar will let you set limits for each answer option.

What happens when a limit is reached?

Once a choice reaches its response limit, it is automatically removed from the form so new respondents can no longer select it.

Does it work with multiple-choice and checkbox questions?

Yes, Choice Limiter 365 works with multiple-choice (radio) and checkbox grid question types in Google Forms.

Is my data safe?

Yes. The add-on only accesses the form you have open and does not store respondent data. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
